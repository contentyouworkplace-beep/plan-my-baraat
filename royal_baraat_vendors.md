## Part I: Core Royal Baraat Vendors

This section catalogs the vendor specializations, services, and assets required to deliver a premium royal wedding procession experience.

### 1. Entry & Procession
*   **Royal Double Decker Bus:** Open-roof buses for guest procession boarding.
*   **Vintage Cars:** Classic historical models for the groom's entourage.
*   **Horse (Ghodi):** Specially decorated white horses for the groom.
*   **Elephant Entry:** Majestic decorated elephants for royal entries.
*   **Camel Entry:** Traditional desert-themed camels.
*   **Luxury Chariot (Baggi):** Horse-drawn floral carriages.
*   **Royal Bike Entry:** Cruisers/bullets with customized entries.
*   **ATV / Jeep Entry:** Rugged or themed off-road vehicle entries.

### 2. Music & Entertainment
*   **Band Party:** Traditional brass bands.
*   **Dhol Team:** Energetic percussionists.
*   **Nashik Dhol:** High-rhythm drums from Maharashtra.
*   **Punjabi Dhol:** Traditional bhangra drum beats.
*   **Tasha Group:** High-tempo folk drums.
*   **Live Singers:** Procession folk/modern singers.
*   **DJ Truck:** Mobile sound trucks with lighting rigs.
*   **Shehnai Artists:** Traditional wind instrumentals.
*   **Folk Artists:** Region-specific dancers and singers.
*   **Dance Troupe:** Professional background dancers.

### 3. Groom Royal Styling
*   **Safa / Pagdi:** Professional turban drapers.
*   **Groom Sherwani:** Premium designer wear suppliers.
*   **Mojdi:** Traditional wedding footwear.
*   **Sword (Talwar):** Royal ceremonial swords.
*   **Royal Brooch:** Decorative gemstone accessories.
*   **Floral Jewellery:** Fresh flower styling for pre-wedding.
*   **Mala / Varmala:** Premium rosebud/pearl wedding garlands.

### 4. Decoration & Experience
*   **Floral Decor:** Entrance gates and passage floral arches.
*   **Baraat Lighting:** Mobile light towers and pathway lighting.
*   **LED Dancers:** Costume-lit performance teams.
*   **Cold Pyro Team:** Sparklers for key entrance moments.
*   **Fireworks:** Professional aerial fireworks displays.
*   **Smoke Gun Effects:** Dry-ice cloud generators.
*   **Entry Props:** Thematic flags, torches, and signs.
*   **Umbrella / Chatri Team:** Traditional embroidered fabric umbrellas for guests.

### 5. Guest Experience
*   **Welcome Girls Team:** Traditional flower petal showers.
*   **Hospitality Staff:** Coordination and guest support.
*   **Water & Refreshments:** Procession hydration stalls.
*   **Security Team:** Bouncers and crowd management.
*   **Valet Parking:** Logistics and car managers.
*   **VIP Management:** Dedicated protocol personnel.

### 6. Wedding Production
*   **Photographer:** Traditional and candid photographers.
*   **Cinematographer:** Cinematic video crews.
*   **Drone Team:** Aerial 4K operators.
*   **LED Wall:** Screens for live procession telecasts.
*   **Sound System:** Ground speakers and monitors.
*   **Stage Setup:** Mandaps and platforms.
*   **Generator Vendor:** Mobile silent power backups.

### 7. Luxury Add-ons
*   **Luxury Cars:** Supercars and high-end sedans.
*   **Vintage Car Collection:** Curated vintage fleets.
*   **Royal Palki:** Groom/bride hand-carried palanquins.
*   **Helicopter Entry:** Sky entrances and flower showers.
*   **Royal Guards:** Palace-uniformed security guards.
*   **Live Animals:** Managed horses, camels, and elephants.
*   **Palace Theme Setup:** Thematic decor recreating royal courts.
